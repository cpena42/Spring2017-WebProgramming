Alarak

Welcome to our Alarak guide for Heroes of the Storm. Here you will learn everything you need to know in order to play Alarak in a competitive environment, whether you play on your own or with a team.



Double Cross Build

Level 1 Level 4 Level 7 Level 10 Level 13 Level 16 Level 20

The Double Cross Build is made up of Talents that focus on increasing the reliability and damage of your combo as much as possible. Use Discord Strike Icon Discord Strike with Telekinesis Icon Telekinesis whenever you can do so safely. Although you are not taking Talents to empower Lightning Surge, use it as often as you can to poke enemy Heroes. Once they are low on Health you can secure kills with Deadly Charge Icon Deadly Charge and Discord Strike Icon Discord Strike, followed by Basic Attacks and your other Abilities. At Level 20 you can repeat your combo by activating Hasty Bargain Icon Hasty Bargain, which should result in a takedown when executed correctly.


Lightning Surge Build

Level 1 Level 4 Level 7 Level 10 Level 13 Level 16 Level 20

The Lightning Surge Build is exceptionally good at long range poke, especially against melee heavy team compositions. It is very easy to play, as you simply use Lightning Surge every time it is available. When you finish stacking Endless Energy Icon Endless Energy, you will be able to use it every 4 seconds. You will outrange most Heroes in the game, and trade well with those who can match you. Target enemy Heroes behind the enemy frontline whenever possible, to damage as many Heroes with each cast as possible.
