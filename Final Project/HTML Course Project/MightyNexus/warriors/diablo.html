Diablo

Welcome to our Diablo guide for Heroes of the Storm. Here you will learn everything you need to know in order to play Diablo in a competitive environment, whether you play on your own or with a team.



Shadow Charge Build

Level 1 Level 4 Level 7 Level 10 Level 13 Level 16 Level 20

This Shadow Charge Icon Shadow Charge Build focuses on improving Shadow Charge to improve Diablo's ability to initiate and survivability. Key Talents are Bulwark Icon Bulwark, which reliably grants a lengthy Resistant effect, Diabolical Momentum Icon Diabolical Momentum, which greatly reduces the cooldown of Shadow Charge and Overpower Icon Overpower, and Domination Icon Domination, which allows Overpower to reset the cooldown of Shadow Charge. These talents synergise well with each other to make Diablo difficult to peel or otherwise kill and very disruptive due to the amount of crowd control he has at his disposal.
