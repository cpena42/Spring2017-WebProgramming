Auriel

Welcome to our Auriel guide for Heroes of the Storm. Here you will learn everything you need to know in order to play Auriel in a competitive environment, whether you play on your own or with a team.



Sacred Sweep Damage Build

Level 1 Level 4 Level 7 Level 10 Level 13 Level 16 Level 20

This build focuses on dealing AoE with Sacred Sweep Icon Sacred Sweep. It is extremely effective for clearing Minions, and will apply noticeable map pressure to games where it is used. This build will also help Auriel deal increased damage in team fights, increasing her healing in turn.


Sacred Sweep Blind Build

Level 1 Level 4 Level 7 Level 10 Level 13 Level 16 Level 20

While also built around Sacred Sweep Icon Sacred Sweep, this build sacrifices damage to allow Auriel to reliably apply Blind to enemy Heroes. It can cripple team compositions that rely on Basic Attacks for most of their damage.


Detainment Strike Build

Level 1 Level 4 Level 7 Level 10 Level 13 Level 16 Level 20

Heavy Burden Icon Heavy Burden combined with the increased push range provided by Repelling Strike Icon Repelling Strike can often result in picks for Auriel's team. This build is best when Auriel has a strong frontline, as healing those allies will also cause damage to the enemy Heroes next to them.
