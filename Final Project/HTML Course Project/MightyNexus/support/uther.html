Uther

Welcome to our Uther guide for Heroes of the Storm. Here you will learn everything you need to know in order to play Uther in a competitive environment, whether you play on your own or with a team.



Holy Radiance Build

Level 1 Level 4 Level 7 Level 10 Level 13 Level 16 Level 20

The Holy Radiance Icon Holy Radiance build seeks to provide Uther with as much uptime as possible on Devotion Icon Devotion's Armor increasing effect. Key Talents include Wave of Light Icon Wave of Light, which improves the duration of the effect on top of greatly reducing the Cooldown of Holy Radiance, and Benediction Icon Benediction, which allows Uther to quickly recast the Ability. By nature, this build thrives against burst damage.
